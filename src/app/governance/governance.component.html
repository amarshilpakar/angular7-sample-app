Governance

To observe good corporate governance practice in Rovio Entertainment Corporation’s decision-making and corporate governance, we commit to Finnish legislation, in particular the Limited Liability Companies Act, Accounting Act and Securities Markets Act, as well as Rovio’s Articles of Association and corporate governance principles ratified by the company’s Board of Directors. We also observe the rules and regulations of the Helsinki Stock Exchange (NASDAQ Helsinki) and the Financial Supervisory Authority, the Corporate Governance Code for Finnish Listed Companies (2015) and the obligations of the market abuse regulation. The Corporate Governance Code can be read on pages https://cgfinland2.fi/en/
