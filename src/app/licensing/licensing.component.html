Licensees

The Angry Birds brand has always been about vivid, fun characters, with a playful history of wandering far outside the original game and into the physical world.

The Angry Birds licensing program, encompassing everything from plush toys to live-action slingshot games, is a thriving business with more than 400 licensees around the world. Interested in teaming up with us to extend the Angry Birds brand experience in fun and cool ways? Contact your local licensing agent!

 

Products

We work hand in hand with licensees to produce consumer products that stay true to the Angry Birds brand experience and delight our fans.

We also work to ensure the safety and quality of our branded products, as well as their safe and ethical manufacture. Rovio and its licensees are committed to the UN Universal Declaration of Human Rights and the Core Conventions of the International Labor Organization.
