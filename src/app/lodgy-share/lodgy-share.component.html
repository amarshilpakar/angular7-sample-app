Rovio’s share (ROVIO) is listed on NASDAQ Helsinki. The company's shares are included in the book-entry system operated by the Euroclear Finland Ltd.

Dividend

Rovio’s intention is to distribute approximately 30 percent of annual net profit excluding items affecting comparability as dividend and equity returns to Rovio’s shareholders and taking other factors such as Rovio’s financial position, cash flow and growth opportunities into consideration.

There can be no assurances that dividends or equity returns will actually be paid in the future, nor are there any assurances as to the amount of dividends or equity returns to be paid for a particular year.

Stock Options

Rovio has established several stock option programs as part of the Rovio Group’s incentive and commitment program for personnel. The purpose of the stock options is to encourage personnel to work on a long-term basis to increase shareholder value. The option rights will be granted to personnel in a way determined by the Board of Directors.

Share subscription with Rovio Entertainment’s option rights 2015A and 2015AII

Currently the share subscription price for the stock options 2015A is 2,76 € per share and the stock options 2015AII 3,00 € per share.

The share subscription period for stock options 2015A and 2015AII is 1 August 2017 – 31 July 2019.

Option holders who have a book-entry account at Evli Bank Plc

Option holders who are currently employed to Rovio Entertainment Corporation and who have a book-entry account at Evli Bank can carry out their share subscription electronically through the Incentive portal at https://incentive.eam.fi.

Other option rights holders (investors) who have a book-entry account at Evli Bank can subscribe shares by contacting Evli Bank’s Helpdesk by email operations@evli.com or by phone +358 (0) 9 4766 9931 (9:00 am – 4:00 pm).

Option holders who have a book-entry account in another account operator

Option holders who are currently employed to Rovio Entertainment Corporation or other option rights holders (investors) who have a book-entry account in another account operator will contact their respective account operator for share subscription. The account operator will provide further information on how to subscribe shares.

After the subscription and paying the subscription price according to the payment instructions the account operator will take care of the required entries in your book-entry account, whereby the options used for subscription will be removed and new shares of Rovio Entertainment Corporation will be entered.

The subscribed shares are not immediately available for selling on the stock exchange, but the new shares will first be entered into the Trade Register and after that the shares will be applied for listing on the stock exchange as a so-called supplementary lot. Therefore, a stock option holder should be aware that the subscribed share would only be quoted after several weeks from the share subscription.

Shares will establish shareholders rights as of the date of share registration.
